Sausage and Peppers

Sausage and Peppers Over Egg Noodles

This is a classic sausage and peppers dish with an adjustable spice level served over egg noodles. It's a hearty, savory take on the classic sandwich, but feel free to leave out the noodles and throw it on a bun instead!

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 lb Italian sausage (mild, medium, hot, sweet)
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 pepper of another color
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • salt (to taste)
  • 4 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • Italian seasoning (to taste)
  • red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • 1/2 lb egg noodles

Instructions

While you work on the following steps, you may wish to boil water for your noodles. Cook them while you make the rest of the dish for more efficiency!

  1. Heat a couple tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat in a pan that has a lid. Add the sausages and brown them slowly, turning occasionally. Once browned, remove from the pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the onions and peppers. Stir to coat in the oil, sautee. Season as desired. When peppers and onions have a good sear, add the garlic and cook an additional minute.
  3. Deglaze the pan with red wine. Gently scrape the bottom of the pan to loosen any burnt on/brown bits. Cook the wine down by half.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, seasoning, and red pepper flakes (if desired), and stir. Return the sausages to the pan, bring to a simmer. Then reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until the sausages are cooked through. This may take about 20 minutes.
  5. Once cooked, serve over a bed of egg noodles. Enjoy!
